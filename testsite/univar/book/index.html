Overview of the Course

Univariate Statistics and Methodology using R is aimed to teach (or consolidate) fundamental methodological and statistical understanding, and introduce the use of R as a powerful tool for understanding data (not just NHST).

On this page you will find the weekly lab exercises, along with links to useful content online, perhaps walkthroughs. etc. Each week, the solutions will be made available here for the previous weeksâ€™ lab.
