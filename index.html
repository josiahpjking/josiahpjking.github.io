Tasting notes: This medium-bodied blog is made from grapes grown in the hills of Cumbria and bottled in Edinburgh. The initial bouquet is of someone self-conscious who doesn't have anything to write about, and unfortunately this feeling persists throughout. With occasional aromas of psycholinguistics, criminology, and administrative data, the underlying flavours are largely suggestive of statistics and R. A fairly mild finish. Goes well with cheese.
